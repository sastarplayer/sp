Just In: Maritzburg United have signed Leletu Skeleem from Stellenbosch FC.


By SA STARPLAYER Tuesday August-17, 09:00 AM
  Mdunge Skhumbuzo

Maritzburg United have signed the attacking player, Letetu Skeleem (23), from Stellenbosch FC.


According to the media statement released by Maritzburg United, the deal of Skeleem was agreed on terms that Judas Moseamedi will move to Stellenbosch FC. Skeleem have played two seasons in PSL after winning the Glad Africa Championship with Stellenbosch in 2018/2019 season.


As he Spoke with the Maritzburg media department, Steleem said he’s very excited to join the team. “ I've received a nice warm welcome from the players, the coaches and the staff," Skelem told the media.”


"I'm 100 percent fully focussed and happy to work with coach Ernst Middendorp, more especially as an international coach. There's that space for him to teach me more about football. I will do my best for the team to help us get good results. To be in the top eight and to fight for the top four, that's the most important goal for me and also for the team.” Skeleem added.


The other new signings at Maritzburg United, are Alfred Ndengane, Zukile Kewuti, Lifa Hlongwane, Brandon Theron, Richard Zumah, Rowan Human and goalkeeper pair Tato Lesoma and Renaldo Leaner
